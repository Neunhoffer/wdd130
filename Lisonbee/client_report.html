Project Overview

This website was designed to explore the environmental design of The Last of Us Part II, specifically how Naughty Dog transformed real Seattle landmarks into a post-apocalyptic world. The site compares real life locations with their in-game counterparts through images and written descriptions.

The most challenging part of the project was definetly the CSS, I was able to overcome it through having a A.I. tutor help me with some of the logic and also testing it constantly with the live server. I would probably spend less time on some of the formating with the html and leave all of that stuff for the CSS. I feel more confident working in the real world for sure after this.
